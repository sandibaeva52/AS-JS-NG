Flowers

The Most Beautiful Flowers In The World

Bird of Paradise

Actually, bird of paradise is an exotic plant native to South Africa. On blooming, its pretty flowers look exactly like the bird of paradise in flight.This unusually beautiful flower symbolizes the paradise itself.A mature bird of paradise plant bloom from May through September.

Tulips

Located in the Netherlands, this large garden spread across a vast land of 32 hectares. Tulip is such a beautiful flower that could instantly grab your attention.The cup-shaped tulip flowers open on each spring. This showy flower comes in all shades of colors except pure blue.

Peony

Peonies are very common flowers in Europe, China and North America. There are almost 40 different species of peonies. They’re so beautiful that they’re usually used alone in bouquets. Like other flowers we’ve mentioned, peonies come in a variety of colors: pink, white, fuchsia, etc.

Gardenias

The gardenia is one of the most beautiful flowers to give as a gift. This flower is originally from China and can be found in a variety of colors. It’s especially known for its subtlety and its bright, smooth leaves.

Hydrangea

This flower is originally from Southern Asia and also is very popular worldwide. It has undeniable beauty and is commonly used in decoration. Also, the hydrangea is an ideal flower for creating bouquets, since it provides lots of volume and texture.
